About

Who we are



How To Change A Life is a collection of resources and people to help you change your life and the lives of others.


Use this as a framework and source of ideas but also a community to share your progress and the impact it makes.


Remember, those small changes build into something huge.




Why we do it



Why create a website when people can search for things themselves?


Well, HTCAL is a dynamic website, that means that you make it better. We are constantly learning from our community about new ways to make a change.


These human stories are helping to build out a community of change makers and by tracking the change we are making we are encouraging even more change.
